  Examples:
  - "Teach me Docker interactively the same way you taught me git"
  - "Teach me SQL interactively — hands on, one task at a time"
  - "Teach me Python basics interactively with muscle memory practice"

  The key parts to include in your prompt:
  1. "one task at a time" — keeps it hands-on
  2. "I'll paste output" — makes it interactive
  3. "test me before moving on" — ensures you actually learned before advancing
  4. "muscle memory practice" — signals you want repetition not just theory

  That's it. Works for anything — Linux commands, Docker, SQL, Python, React, AWS, etc.
